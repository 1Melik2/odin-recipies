Adana Kebab

Description

Adana kebab is a signature dish of Turkish cuisine, originating from the city of Adana in southern Turkey. Renowned for its bold flavors and tantalizing aroma, this dish is a must-try for meat lovers who appreciate the perfect balance of spice and smokiness.

Adana kebab is a feast for the senses. The spicy, smoky aroma whets the appetite, while the juicy meat delivers a satisfying burst of flavor with every bite. Paired with a refreshing drink like ayran (a yogurt-based beverage), it offers a perfect balance of heat and coolness. Whether enjoyed at a Turkish restaurant or as street food in the vibrant streets of Adana, this dish is a testament to the rich culinary heritage of Turkey.

Ingredients

  • Ground Lamb or Beef 500g
  • Red Pepper Paste 1tbsp
  • Finley grated medium onion
  • 1tsp of following spices
    • Red Pepper Flakes
    • Paprika
    • Garlic
    • Salt
    • Black Pepper
  • Lavash Bread
  • Grilled vegetables
  • Lemon wedges
  • Grilled vegetables
  • Parsley and onion salad

Steps

  1. Finley grate the onion
  2. Mix all spices and grated onion
  3. Add meat with spices and knead the meat for 10 minutes
  4. Chill the mixture in fridge for at least 30 minutes
  5. Shape the meat onto flat metal skewers
  6. Grill kebab for 3-4 minute on each side until outside is slightly charred
  7. Grill vegetables such as green peppers, tomatoes, and onions
  8. Place lavash bread on platter with kebab, vegetables, lemon juice, and salad on top
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
