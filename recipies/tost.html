Tost

Description

Turkish tost is a popular and versatile snack or light meal enjoyed across Turkey. It is essentially a grilled sandwich, but its unique preparation, ingredients, and taste make it stand out from other variations of toast worldwide.

Turkish tost is beloved for its simplicity, flavor, and ability to be customized to personal preferences. Whether enjoyed as a quick breakfast, a street food snack, or a midnight craving, it remains a staple of Turkish cuisine and a must-try for anyone exploring Turkish flavors.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Cheese (preferably mozzarella or Turkish ka≈üar)
  • Sliced Sucuk (Turkish sausage)
  • Vegetables or anything else
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients
    • Slice the sucuk thinly
    • If adding vegetables, slice them into thin pieces.
  2. Butter the bread
  3. Assemble to sandwich together
    • Lay cheese on bread
    • Put sucuk and toppings on top
  4. Grill on panini press or pan
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
