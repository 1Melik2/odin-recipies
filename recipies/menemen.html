Menemen

Description

Menemen is a beloved and hearty Turkish breakfast dish made with eggs, tomatoes, peppers, and a medley of spices. It's simple to prepare, yet incredibly flavorful, making it a staple in Turkish households and breakfast tables. The dish is traditionally served in a pan and enjoyed communally, often with fresh bread to scoop up its saucy, savory goodness.

Menemen strikes a perfect balance between simplicity and flavor. Its warm, comforting texture and vibrant colors evoke the cozy feeling of a leisurely Turkish breakfast. The combination of eggs with the fresh, slightly tangy tomatoes and mild peppers creates a dish that's both satisfying and refreshing. Whether enjoyed as part of an elaborate Turkish breakfast spread or on its own as a quick, wholesome meal, menemen is a true classic that reflects the warmth and hospitality of Turkish cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 2-3 grated medium tomatoes
  • 2 mild green peppers
  • 2 tsp olive oil and butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Bread
  • Optional red pepper flakes
  • Optional kaşar or mozzarella cheese
  • Optional Turkish sausage

Steps

  1. Finley grate tomatoes and slice peppers
  2. Add pepper and olive oil to medium hot pan and sauté for 2-3 minutes
  3. Add grated tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Add seasoning
  5. Add whisked or cracked eggs into pan
  6. Add optional cheese or sausage
  7. Grill vegetables such as green peppers, tomatoes, and onions
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
